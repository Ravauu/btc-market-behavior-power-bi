# BTC Market Behavior Dashboard

Power BI project analyzing BTC 5-minute market behavior using Binance perpetual futures data.

The dashboard focuses on intraday volatility, candle structure, trading activity, taker buy share and differences between market sessions.

## Project Overview

The dataset contains approximately 139,680 five-minute BTCUSD perpetual candles from January 2025 to April 2026.

The project was created to explore how BTC behaves across different hours, days of the week and trading sessions. The analysis is focused on market behavior and descriptive analytics, not on generating trading signals.

## What the Dashboard Shows

The dashboard shows:

- how BTC 5-minute volatility changes throughout the day
- differences in market behavior between Asia, London, New York and AfterHours sessions
- average candle range, body size and close position
- changes in taker buy share across different hours
- trading activity measured by quote volume and trade count
- the distribution of Directional, Mixed and Indecision candles
- differences in average quote volume between days of the week
- combinations of day and hour with higher or lower average 5-minute range
- the share of candles where taker buy volume is above 50%
